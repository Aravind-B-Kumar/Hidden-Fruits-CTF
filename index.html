Some common questions
were often asked

Why are certain animal species endangered?

Endangerment can result from habitat loss, poaching, climate change, and pollution.

What can I do to help protect wildlife?

You can support conservation efforts, reduce plastic use, and promote habitat restoration.

How do I attract wildlife to my garden?

Plant native species, provide water sources, and create shelter for various animals.

Why is biodiversity important?

Biodiversity ensures ecosystem stability, supports food webs, and provides genetic resources.

How are conservation areas chosen?

Areas are selected based on biodiversity hotspots, the presence of endangered species, and ecological significance.

What are the threats to marine life?

Marine life faces threats from overfishing, pollution, habitat destruction, and climate change.
